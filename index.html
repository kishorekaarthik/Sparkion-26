About the Event

What is SPARKION'26?

SPARKION'26 is a national-level symposium hosted by the Department of Electrical & Electronics Engineering and the Society of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (SEEE) at GCE Tirunelveli. Students from across India compete, showcase their skills, and win prizes — in a fun, energetic environment.

Whether you love coding, quizzes, photography, music, or storytelling — there's an event built just for you. Register for just ₹250 and be part of the excitement!

Compete & Win

Events

Idea Spark

Got a cool idea? Pitch it to a panel and compete for the best innovation award.

Code Clash

A programming competition where speed and accuracy decide who wins.

Tech Talk

Present a topic you're passionate about. Judged on knowledge, clarity, and confidence.

Electro Quiz

A fast buzzer quiz on electrical engineering concepts. Test your knowledge!

Circuit Hunt

A fun treasure hunt with engineering clues. Think fast and win big!

Cine Quiz

A buzzer quiz on Tamil and Indian cinema — songs, actors, and iconic dialogues.

Visual Vault

Spot hidden images and solve visual puzzles. Sharp eyes and quick thinking win!

Paatu Paada Vaa

Guess the Tamil song by their background music and pictures. Fun, musical and full of energy!

Minutes Mystery

60 seconds to memorize. 60 seconds to recall. An intense and fun memory battle.

Clue the View

Identify famous places from clever visual clues. Great for travel lovers!

Photography

Meme Creation

Short Film

Submit a short film or acting scene. Tell a great story and showcase your talent.
