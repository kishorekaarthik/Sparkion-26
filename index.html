About the Event

What is SPARKION'26?

SPARKION'26 is a national-level symposium hosted by the Society of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (SEEE) at GCE Tirunelveli. Students from across India compete, showcase their skills, and win prizes — in a fun, energetic environment.

Whether you love coding, quizzes, photography, music, or storytelling — there's an event built just for you. Register for just ₹250 and be part of the excitement!
